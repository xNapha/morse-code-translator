Guidelines for using the translator

  • When entering morse code, please:
    • space your letters apart with a single space
    • e.g. ".... . .-.. .-.. ---"
      •
    • space your words apart with three spaces
    • e.g. "... - --- .--.   .-.. . .- ...- ."
  • If an english word comes before or after the start of a morse code word seperate them with three spaces
    • e.g. "over here   .-.. . .- ...- ."
      •
    • e.g. ".-.. . .- ...- .   no over here"
  • Translator does not handle the following characters when used in conjunction with english:
    • "Super-hero" will throw an error
      •
    • "Have a good day." will throw an error
      •
